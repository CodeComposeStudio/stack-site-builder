Hi. This site is an "awesome stack" catalog built with the
[**stack-site-builder**](https://github.com/CodeCompose7/stack-site-builder)
theme. It collects tools and keeps their detail pages, concepts, articles and
slides in one place.

This **About** page has no listing of its own — it renders as a single page and
shows up as one item in the header navigation, a top-level section just like
concepts, articles or samples.

## What this site covers

Content is split into catalog entries (stacks), articles, concepts and
presentation slides. On top of those you can add standalone pages (like this
one) that stand on their own with no index.

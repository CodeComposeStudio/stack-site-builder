## Why it matters \{#why}

Replaced recurrence with self-attention, enabling parallel training and long-range
dependencies — the architecture behind every modern LLM.

## Key ideas \{#key-ideas}

- Scaled dot-product attention and multi-head attention
- Positional encodings instead of recurrence
- Encoder–decoder stacks built purely from attention and feed-forward layers
